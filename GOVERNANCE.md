# Governance

## Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for roadmap decisions, architecture direction, security response, and release quality.

## Decision-Making

- Day-to-day technical decisions: maintainers
- Breaking changes: require maintainer consensus in PR discussion
- Security fixes: handled privately, then disclosed after patch

## Contribution Flow

- Community submits issues/PRs
- Maintainers review for correctness, scope, and quality
- Approved PRs are merged by maintainers

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors with sustained, high-quality contributions may be invited by existing maintainers.
